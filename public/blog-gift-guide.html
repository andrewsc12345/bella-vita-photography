Gift Guide

The Perfect Gift for Seniors & Families

By Rebecca Henson · March 2026 · 5 min read

Stuck on what to give the person who has everything? A photography gift is personal, meaningful, and something they'll keep forever. Here are my favorite gift ideas for every budget and occasion — from a $50 print to a full session experience.

Gift Certificates

A gift certificate for a photo session is one of the most thoughtful gifts you can give. You're not giving someone a thing — you're giving them an experience and a set of memories they'll have forever. It's the kind of gift that gets better with time.

Gift certificates come in any amount and never expire. You can cover a full session, a mini session, or just contribute toward one. I'll send a beautifully designed digital certificate straight to your inbox (or theirs) that's ready to print or forward.

This is the number one gift that grandparents buy for their kids and grandkids. It's also popular for birthdays, Mother's Day, Father's Day, and graduation. Every year I get messages saying "this was the best gift I've ever received" — and I never get tired of hearing it.

Rebecca's tip: If you're buying for someone specific, let me know and I can include a personal note on the certificate. A message like "For Sarah's senior portraits — Love, Grandma and Grandpa" makes it even more special.

Canvas Wraps

If someone in your life already has gorgeous photos but hasn't printed them (and let's be honest, most of us are guilty of this), a canvas wrap is the gift that gets those images off the phone and onto the wall where they belong.

Canvas wraps are museum-quality, ready to hang, and they make any room feel warmer and more personal. The texture and depth of a canvas print is completely different from a flat paper print — it looks and feels like art.

I can work with existing photos from a previous bella vita session or help you choose the right image if they've had a session recently. Popular sizes are 16x20 for a statement piece and 11x14 for a smaller accent. Galleries of multiple canvas wraps in different sizes look incredible as a wall arrangement.

Custom Albums

Albums are the ultimate heirloom gift. I design each one by hand, selecting the best images and laying them out on thick, lay-flat pages with leather or linen covers. When you hold one, you know immediately that it's something special.

Albums are perfect for grandparents, for parents of graduating seniors, and as anniversary gifts. They're also the product that clients tell me they're most glad they ordered, because the experience of flipping through a physical album is completely different from scrolling through a phone.

I can design an album from any past bella vita session. If the photos were taken years ago, that's fine — the files are archived and ready to go. An album of their favorite family photos or their child's senior portraits makes a gift they'll reach for over and over.

Gift Ideas by Occasion

Graduation

A senior portrait session gift certificate is the most popular graduation gift I see. Parents or grandparents cover the session, and the graduate gets a professional portfolio of photos to mark the milestone. Alternatively, a canvas wrap of their favorite senior portrait for their college dorm room is always a hit.

Mother's Day & Father's Day

A family session gift certificate is a gift for the whole family, but especially for the parent who always says "we need to get family photos done" but never books it. Giving them the session takes the planning off their plate — all they have to do is show up.

Christmas & Holidays

Gift certificates, canvas wraps, and albums are all popular holiday gifts. If you're ordering a physical product, plan ahead — albums take 3–4 weeks to produce, and the holiday rush adds time. For last-minute gifts, a digital gift certificate is instant and always well-received.

Birthdays & Anniversaries

A couples session or family session gift certificate works beautifully for milestone birthdays and anniversaries. For someone who recently had a baby, a newborn or milestone session gift gives them professional photos during a time when they're too exhausted to plan it themselves.

Rebecca's tip: The best photography gifts are the ones that remove the barriers. Most people want professional photos but never get around to booking, planning, or paying. A gift certificate handles all of that. All they have to do is pick a date.

Ideas by Budget

Under $100

A gift certificate for any amount toward a future session or product order. Even $50 or $75 toward a session is meaningful and appreciated. Fine art prints in smaller sizes also fall in this range.

$100 – $250

A mini session gift certificate (covers the full mini session cost), a canvas wrap in a popular size, or a contribution toward a full session. This is the sweet spot for a generous, thoughtful gift without a major investment.

$250 – $500

A full session gift certificate, a custom album, or a canvas wrap gallery set. This range covers the complete experience — the kind of gift that creates memories and produces photos they'll treasure for a lifetime.

$500+

The full experience: a session gift certificate paired with a canvas wrap or album credit. This is the ultimate photography gift and it's popular for milestone occasions like graduations, significant birthdays, and major anniversaries.
