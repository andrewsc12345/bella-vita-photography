Client Gallery Guide
— for Rebecca —

1To publish a new gallery

  1. Export from Lightroom. Select your finished photos. File → Export with your standard web preset (JPEG, ~2000px long edge, watermark on). Export to a folder — Desktop works fine.
  2. Double-click "Publish Gallery" on your desktop.
  3. Pick the folder you just exported.
  4. Type the client's name — e.g., "Smith Family" or "Presley Johnson". This shows on the gallery card.
  5. Set a password. Keep it simple and lowercase. Clients will need to type it.
  6. Pick the session type from the dropdown.
  7. Review and click Publish. Wait about 2 minutes. A "Gallery published" message confirms when it's done.
  8. Share the password with your client and point them to bellavitabyrebecca.com → Client Gallery → their session.

2To delete an old gallery

Important: Deleting permanently removes the gallery from your website. Your original photos in Lightroom are safe — but the published version is gone until republished. Only delete when you're sure.
  1. Double-click "Delete Gallery" on your desktop.
  2. Pick the gallery from the dropdown.
  3. Read the confirmation and click Delete to confirm.

3If something goes wrong

  1. Don't try to fix it yourself. Take a screenshot of any error message that appears (Cmd+Shift+4, drag a box around the error).
  2. Text Chris with the screenshot and what you were doing. He can fix anything within a few minutes, no matter what.
